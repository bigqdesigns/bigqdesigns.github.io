Get To Know Me - 2000's style


Does anyone else remember those list of questions that went around in the early 2000's? Figured that's a fun way to meet me - the guy behind bigQDesigns.

1. Where did you grow up? Rural Ohio.. not exactly the most Queer friendly place.. but I made it out.
2. Where do you live now? Palm Springs, California. Yes - it's hot in the summer. Yes - it's mostly older people. Yes - I love it.
3. Dogs or Cats? Both! Currently I have a cat named Felicia.
4. Favorite Drink? Lemondrop Martini - a classic that is pretty hard to mess up.
5. Tea of Coffee? Coffee - preferably iced with milk/splenda.
6. Which languages do you speak? English currently - attempting to learn german. "Kaffee und milch, bitte!"
7. Favorite Movie? The Wizard of Oz - forever and always. As a child I only watched 3 films in rotation and this was one of them.
8. Favorite Drag Anthem? Proud by Heather Small - it was the first drag performance I'd ever seen.
9. Favorite Queer Bar? Bounce in Cleveland was where I had my Queer coming of age - unfortunately it's closed.
10. Why bigQDesigns? It's a play on words from one of my favorite TV shows - Queer as Folk.
